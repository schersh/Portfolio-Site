Clean Slate:

Get even with your roommates, and live happily ever after. An app for settling your accounts.

"Clean Slate" is an app built with Ruby on Rails to allow roommates to track household expenses over the course of a month, and then use a "settle up" function at the end of the month. This app makes cost-sharing more organized and reduces the time and energy roommates need to devote to tallying up expenses and splitting them evenly among household members.
